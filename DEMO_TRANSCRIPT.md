## [CREATING A CLAIM — 0:18]

*[Screen shows the New Claim form]*

"An adjuster opens a new claim, uploads the incident form and the policy PDF, and clicks Create.

*[Loading spinner appears]*

In the background, our first AI agent — FNOLTriageAgent — is reading both documents and producing a coverage recommendation.

*[Spinner disappears — Triage tab shows a green COVERED badge at 94% confidence]*

94% confidence. Covered. Matched clauses, no exclusions, no risk flags. What used to take an adjuster 20 minutes of policy reading — done in seconds.

The adjuster reviews the result and clicks Approve."

---

## [WORKSHOP & WORK ORDER — 0:50]

*[Screen shows the Taller tab — single button visible]*

"The claim moves to the workshop. The vehicle arrives, the technician marks it as received, uploads inspection photos, and clicks Generate Work Order.

*[Loading spinner]*

Our second agent — WorkOrderDraftAgent — analyses the photos using AI vision and generates a full structured work order: replacement, repair severity, painting, external work — line by line.

*[Work Order tab shows table of line items]*

The adjuster approves each item. Then the workshop submits their budget PDF.

*[Loading spinner — reconciliation agent runs]*

Agent number three — WorkOrderReconciliationAgent — fuzzy-matches the budget against the approved scope, fills in all costs, and immediately flags one item the workshop added that wasn't in the original scope.

*[⚠️ row visible in the table]*

The adjuster rejects it. Repair begins."

---

## [CUSTOMER PORTAL — 1:45]

*[Toggle switches to customer view — Estado tab shows visual timeline]*

"Meanwhile — this is what the customer sees.

A live timeline. Green for done, orange for current. Right now it shows 'Repair in progress' with today's date. No phone calls, no waiting on hold.

When the repair is complete and the vehicle is ready, the customer sees it here immediately — and gets a prompt to come collect their car."

---

## [CLOSING THE CLAIM — 2:10]

*[Handler view — Taller tab shows delivery section]*

"Back on the handler side. The customer collects their car. The workshop uploads the signed conformity receipt. One click — the claim moves to billing.

*[Work Order tab — bill upload section visible]*

The workshop submits their final invoice. Our fourth agent — BillComparisonAgent — compares it line by line against the approved work order.

*[Comparison table appears with one ⚠️ flag]*

One line is $120 over what was approved. Flagged instantly, with the exact discrepancy and reason.

The adjuster reviews, adds a note, approves.

*[Dashboard shows claim as Completed — green]*

Claim closed."
