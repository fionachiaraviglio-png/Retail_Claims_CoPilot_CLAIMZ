🚀 Key improvements introduced by ClearProcess

AI triage in seconds: coverage decision with confidence score and matched clauses — previously hours of manual policy review
Defined SLAs at every stage with automatic tracking — previously no visibility into per-stage processing times
Work Order auto-generated from inspection photos by AI — previously the adjuster built it manually line by line
Automatic budget reconciliation: AI fuzzy-matches workshop quote against approved WO — previously done manually and error-prone
AI invoice verification: detects unapproved items, cost overruns and discrepancies automatically before payment
Real-time customer portal: policyholder tracks status, uploads documents and receives updates — previously phone and email only
Full process staging: every stage has defined inputs, outputs and owners — previously the workflow was informal and undocumented

📊 Traditional process vs. ClearProcess

❌ Traditional process (today)
No triage — adjuster reads policy documents manually
No staging — process advances informally with no structure
No SLAs — no visibility into time spent at each stage
Work Order built manually — hours of adjuster time per claim
Budget compared manually — frequent errors and overcharges
No customer portal — all communication via phone and email
Invoice reviewed manually — no anomaly detection before payment

FNOLTriageAgent: coverage decision + confidence score in seconds
10 defined stages with inputs, outputs and clear ownership
Automatic KPIs: SLA alerts triggered at every stage transition
